ABout Vi van

Acen is a private caterer company offers fine dining to your home! Vivian Andersen, a professional chef from Uganda, Africa creates the most exclusive fine dining restaurant experience in Denmark. Her life-long experience prepared her to bring special tastes in her cuisine. She specialised for French dishes, but she has no limitations in cooking. Contact her today!
